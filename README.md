# Pokédex Pro

**Live app:** [pokedexproapp.netlify.app](https://pokedexproapp.netlify.app)

Your friendly, no-frills Pokédex for building the perfect team. Browse all Pokémon, search by name, and pick your favorites — then see your squad's full stats and type breakdown all in one place.

---

## What you can do

- **Browse the full Pokédex** — every Pokémon, right at your fingertips
- **Search by name** — find that one Pokémon you're thinking of, fast
- **Build a team of up to 6** — tap any Pokémon to add it to your team
- **See detailed stats** — HP, Attack, Defense, and more for each member
- **Get a team summary** — total weight, average height, total HP, and a type count so you can spot any gaps in your coverage

---

## How to use it

1. Open the app and browse or search for Pokémon
2. Click a Pokémon to add it to your team (up to 6)
3. Hit **Create Team** to see your full team breakdown
4. Hit **← Back** to adjust your roster and try again

That's it. Have fun!
